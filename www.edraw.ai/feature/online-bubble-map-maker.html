How to create a bubble map using Edraw.AI?

Select a template
On the homepage, head to the Templates section, and select Mind Map from the Diagram Type. Browse through various templates and pick a bubble map that suits your needs. Select Create with Template to start customizing.
Edit and customize
Edit your bubble map by dragging and dropping icons and shapes from the Clipart section to personalize each element. Modify the layout via the right-side Canvas customization panel, or ask Edraw AI chat for quick content tips and support.
Export your bubble map!
Once your bubble map is complete, navigate to the Export option on the quick-access toolbar to save it in your choice of format—PDF, PNG, Excel, etc. Alternatively, use the Share feature in the top-right corner to create a link and collaborate with your team.
