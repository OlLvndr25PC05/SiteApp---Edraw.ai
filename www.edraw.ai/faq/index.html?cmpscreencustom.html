FAQs about Edraw.AI

  • What's the relationship between Edraw.AI, EdrawMax, EdrawMind, and Edrawsoft?
    Edraw.AI, EdrawMax, and EdrawMind are all products of Edrawsoft. Edraw.AI is a purely cloud-based app, while EdrawMind and EdrawMax are accessible on the desktop and online. To use their online version, please visit EdrawMind Online and EdrawMax Online.
  • Can I open emmx. and eddx. files on Edraw.AI?
    Yes, Edraw.AI is compatible with EdrawMax and EdrawMind, which means you can import files created by EdrawMax and EdrawMind on Edraw.AI and edit them.
  • Can I use premium functions of Edraw.AI after buying a license of EdrawMind or EdrawMax?
    Edraw.AI, Edrawmind, and EdrawMax are separate products, which means you cannot use premium features of Edraw.AI simply because you have bought licenses of EdrawMind or EdrawMax.
