This template presents a Porter's Five Forces analysis for an M - Health network. The "Threats of new entrants" include the need for specialist staff training on new networks and device software updates. "Competitive rivalry" is growing as more healthcare facilities adopt M - Health and focus on security. "Bargaining power" of buyers is seen in hospitals using M - Health, with a unique selling point of direct doctor - network connection. "Supplier power" emphasizes the importance of a large hospital volume on the cloud for productivity. "Threats of substitute" mention cloud computing's potential to connect healthcare facilities, with significant change costs but potential productivity gains. This analysis helps in understanding the competitive landscape and strategic considerations for the M - Health network in the healthcare industry.
