This template is a useful reference chart for water treatment symbols. It lists the "Symbols Name" and shows the corresponding "Symbols" for different water - treatment components. For example, the symbol for a "Water Filter" is depicted as a simple vertical structure, while the "Central Water Purifier" has a distinct cylindrical symbol. The chart also includes symbols for "Clarifier", "Containment Basin", and "Covered dome tank" on one side, and "Flume", "Fluted Elevated Tank", "Oil - water Separator" on the other. Additionally, symbols for "Primary Clarifier", "Treatment Basin", "Cooling Tower", and "Evaporation" are provided. This chart is valuable for engineers, designers, and professionals in the water - treatment industry to ensure clear and consistent communication in diagrams and plans.
